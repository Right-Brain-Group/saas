### Client Overview

**Malvern Buttery** is a beloved local eatery offering a delightful array of coffee, pastries, breads, soups, salads, sandwiches, and more. Located in Malvern, their welcoming atmosphere and commitment to quality make them a favorite spot for both locals and visitors.

### Scope - Branding

Our project with Malvern Buttery aimed to encapsulate their commitment to comfort, quality, and community through comprehensive branding. We worked closely with their team to understand their vision and translate it into a compelling brand identity.

#### Branding

1. **Logo Design**: We crafted a logo that reflects the warm and inviting nature of Malvern Buttery, incorporating elements that symbolize their artisanal approach and community focus. The logo features a clean, modern design with subtle nods to classic bakery aesthetics, creating a timeless appeal.

2. **Color Palette**: The selected colors evoke comfort and freshness. Earthy tones and warm hues were chosen to create a cozy, welcoming atmosphere both online and in-store. The color palette was designed to reflect the natural, wholesome ingredients used in their offerings.

3. **Typography**: We chose fonts that are modern yet approachable, ensuring they are easy to read across various mediums, enhancing the user experience. The typography complements the brand’s friendly and artisanal vibe, balancing professionalism with a touch of warmth.

4. **Brand Voice**: Collaborating with the Malvern Buttery team, we established a brand voice that is warm, inviting, and community-oriented. This voice reflects their dedication to quality and customer care, emphasizing their commitment to creating a welcoming environment for all guests.

5. **Visual Elements**: High-quality images and graphics were created to convey the sense of comfort and quality that Malvern Buttery is known for. We used professional food photography to highlight their menu items, ensuring that their visual content is as appealing and appetizing as their physical offerings.

### Highlights

- **Comprehensive Branding**: From logo design to color palette and typography, we developed a cohesive brand identity that reflects Malvern Buttery’s values and vision.
- **Engaging Content**: Carefully curated visual and written content that highlights Malvern Buttery’s commitment to quality and community.
- **Aesthetic Design**: High-quality images and graphics that convey a sense of comfort and artisanal quality.

### A Partnership of Comfort and Innovation

Working with Malvern Buttery has been a rewarding experience.
